### **Q17: Finding a reliable house cleaning service?**
**Steps:**
1. **Referrals**: Ask neighbors or local Facebook groups for vetted providers.

### **Q18: Selecting a local daycare for a toddler?**
**Critical factors:**
- **Licensing**: Verify state compliance (e.g., [ChildCare.gov](https://www.childcare.gov/)).
- **Staff-to-child ratio**: Max 1:4 for toddlers (NAEYC standards).
- **Safety**: Secure entry, CPR-trained staff, and allergy protocols.
- **Curriculum**: Look for play-based learning (e.g., Montessori, Reggio Emilia).
- **Parent reviews**: Visit during operating hours and observe interactions.
*Source: NAEYC, Zero to Three (2024).*

### **Q19: Evaluating a local auto mechanic?**
**Checklist:**
1. **Certifications**: ASE-certified technicians (look for the blue seal).
2. **Specialization**: Ensure they service your car’s make (e.g., **Firestone** for general, **dealerships** for brand-specific).
3. **Warranty**: Minimum 12-month/12,000-mile warranty on parts/labor.
4. **Transparency**: Avoid shops that won’t show you old parts or explain repairs.
5. **Reviews**: Focus on recent Google/Yelp feedback (ignore outliers).
*Source: AAA, Consumer Reports (2024).*
