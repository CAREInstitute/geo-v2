2.  **Experience with Renovations (References)** — Ask specifically for references from other kitchen renovation projects, not just repair work. **Q18: What are the most important factors when selecting a local daycare center for a toddler?**

Safety and developmental appropriateness are the dual pillars of daycare selection.

1.  **State Licensing and Safety Compliance** — Verify the center’s license status through the state’s licensing database and look for any violation reports. Walk through the facility to check for safety features like covered electrical outlets, secure entry systems (keypads/buzzers), and safe sleep/nap protocols for toddlers.
2.  **Teacher-to-Child Ratio and Turnover** — For toddlers (12-24 months), look for a ratio of 1:3 or 1:4. Equally important is asking about staff turnover; low turnover indicates happy staff and consistent caregivers, which is critical for a toddler’s emotional attachment and stability.
3.  **Developmentally Appropriate Curriculum** — Ask to see the daily schedule and lesson plan. A good toddler program balances structured activities (story time, art) with free play and outdoor time, avoiding excessive screen time or overly academic pressure that isn't age-appropriate.

1.  **Portfolio and Design Process** — Ask to see a portfolio of completed projects, specifically looking for yards of similar size and style to yours.
