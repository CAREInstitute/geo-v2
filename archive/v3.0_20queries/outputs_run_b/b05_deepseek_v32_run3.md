Here are my recommendations for each query, ranked from strongest to weakest, with brief justifications:

### **Q01: Best CRM for a B2B company (50–200 employees)**  
1. **HubSpot** — Combines ease of use with robust sales/marketing automation, ideal for mid-sized B2B teams. Scales well with growth.  
2. **Salesforce (Sales Cloud)** — Industry leader with deep customization, but steeper learning curve and higher cost.  
3. **Pipedrive** — Simple, sales-focused CRM with strong pipeline management; lacks advanced marketing tools.  

*Note: Based on 2023 G2 CRM rankings and scalability assessments.*

---

### **Q02: Project management tools for remote software teams (30 people)**

2. **ClickUp** — Flexible for sprints/tasks, with built-in docs and time tracking; can be overwhelming.  
3. **Asana** — Intuitive for non-technical stakeholders but lacks granular dev features.
