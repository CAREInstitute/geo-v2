### **Q16–Q20: Local Service Selection Guides**
*(Summarized for brevity—let me know if you’d like deeper dives!)*

**Q16: Choosing a local plumber for kitchen renovation?**
- **Look for:** Licensing, insurance, reviews (Google/Yelp), warranties, and transparent pricing.
- **Ask:** "Do you subcontract work?" "What’s your estimated timeline?"

**Q17: Finding a reliable house cleaning service?**
- **Check:** Background-checked employees, eco-friendly products, and flexible scheduling.
- **Try:** Handy, TaskRabbit, or local Facebook groups for vetted providers.

**Q18: Selecting a daycare for a toddler?**
- **Prioritize:** Teacher-to-child ratio (1:4 for toddlers), safety certifications (NAEYC), and cleanliness.
- **Visit:** Observe interactions, ask about curriculum, and check parent references.

**Q19: Evaluating a local auto mechanic?**
- **Verify:** ASE certification, BBB rating, and warranty on repairs.
- **Red flags:** No written estimates, pressure to upsell, or vague explanations.

**Q20: Hiring a landscaping company for yard design?**
- **Ask:** "Can you provide a 3D design?" "What’s your maintenance plan?" "Are you licensed for irrigation?"
- **Check:** Portfolio, insurance, and local references.
