**Q01: Best CRM software for a B2B company with 50 to 200 employees**  
1. **HubSpot CRM** — Best overall fit because it scales seamlessly from free to enterprise, offers excellent native marketing-sales alignment, robust reporting, and a modern interface that mid-sized teams adopt quickly without heavy training.  
2. **Salesforce Sales Cloud** — Extremely powerful with unmatched customization and ecosystem of apps; ideal if the company has budget for implementation support and needs advanced forecasting.

HubSpot edges out for most 50–200 employee B2B firms due to lower total cost of ownership and faster time-to-value. (Sources consulted: G2 2025 CRM reports, Capterra user reviews, and vendor pricing pages, December 2025.)

**Q16: What to look for when choosing a local plumber for a kitchen renovation**  
Look for licensed and insured plumbers with specific experience in kitchen remodels (not just emergency service). Check recent online reviews on Google and Nextdoor, verify they pull proper permits, ask for references on similar jobs, and compare detailed written estimates that include fixtures and labor. Strong local reputation and clear communication matter more than the lowest bid. Avoid anyone who pressures you to decide immediately.
