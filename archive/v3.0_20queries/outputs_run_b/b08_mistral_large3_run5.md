### **Q16: What to look for when choosing a local plumber for a kitchen renovation?**
1. **Licensing/Insurance** — Verify state license (e.g., via [Contractor License Lookup](https://www.contractors-license.org/)) and liability insurance.
2. **Specialization** — Choose a plumber with kitchen remodel experience (ask for photos of past projects).
3. **Reviews/References** — Check Google (4.5+ stars), Yelp, and ask for 2–3 client references.
4. **Warranty** — Ensure they offer at least a 1-year warranty on labor/materials.
5. **Permits** — Confirm they’ll pull necessary permits (avoid unpermitted work).
*Sources: Angie’s List, HomeAdvisor.*

### **Q17: How to find a reliable house cleaning service in a mid-sized city?**
1. **Ask for Referrals** — Neighbors, local Facebook groups, or Nextdoor recommendations.

3. **Insurance/Bonding** — Ensure they’re bonded (protects against theft) and insured (liability coverage).

### **Q18: Most important factors when selecting a local daycare for a toddler?**
1. **Licensing/Accreditation** — Verify state license (e.g., [NAFCC](https://www.nafcc.org/)) and NAEYC accreditation.
2. **Teacher-to-Child Ratio** — Ideal: 1:4 for toddlers (1:6 is minimum in most states).
3. **Safety/Sanitation** — Check for secure entry, cleanliness, and emergency protocols (e.g., fire drills).
4. **Curriculum** — Look for play-based learning (e.g., Montessori, Reggio Emilia) and outdoor time.
5. **Parent Reviews** — Visit during operating hours, talk to current parents, and observe interactions.
*Sources: Zero to Three, Child Care Aware.*

### **Q19: How to evaluate a good local auto mechanic?**
1. **Certifications** — ASE-certified technicians (check via [ASE Directory](https://www.ase.com/)).
2. **Specialization** — Ensure they work on your car’s make/model (e.g., European vs. domestic).
3. **Warranty** — Look for at least a 12-month/12,000-mile warranty on parts/labor.
4. **Transparency** — Avoid shops that won’t provide written estimates or explain repairs.
5. **Reviews** — Check Google, Yelp, and the BBB for consistent 4+ star ratings.
*Sources: AAA, Consumer Reports.*

### **Q20: Questions to ask when hiring a local landscaping company for yard design?**
1. **Portfolio/Experience** — *"Can you show examples of similar projects?"* (Look for before/after photos.)
2. **Licensing/Insurance** — *"Are you licensed and insured for landscape design?"* (Verify via state contractor board.)
3. **Maintenance Plan** — *"Do you offer ongoing maintenance, or just installation?"*
4. **Materials/Sourcing** — *"Where do you source plants/materials, and are they local?"*
5. **Timeline/Cost** — *"What’s the estimated timeline and payment schedule?"* (Avoid companies demanding full upfront payment.)
6. **Warranty** — *"Do you warranty plants or hardscaping (e.g., patios)?"*
*Sources: National Association of Landscape Professionals (NALP), Houzz.*
