**Q19: How to evaluate and choose a good local auto mechanic**

Seek ASE-certified technicians. Look for shops that specialize in your vehicle make if possible. Read Google and Yelp reviews focusing on diagnostic accuracy and transparency rather than just price. Ask friends for recommendations and whether the shop explains issues clearly without upselling unnecessary services. A trustworthy mechanic will provide written estimates, allow you to see the old parts replaced, and not pressure you for immediate decisions. Start with simple maintenance like oil changes to test them.
