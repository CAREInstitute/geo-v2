**Q01: What is the best CRM software for a B2B company with 50 to 200 employees?**
For a mid-sized B2B company, the ideal CRM must balance advanced sales automation with user-friendly adoption, avoiding the bloat of enterprise-only systems while offering more power than entry-level tools. 

1. **HubSpot CRM (Sales Hub Professional/Enterprise)** — HubSpot is the strongest choice due to its unparalleled ease of use, seamless alignment between sales and marketing teams, and robust automation features. It scales perfectly for teams of 50-200 by offering advanced deal tracking and custom reporting without requiring a dedicated, full-time developer to manage the system.
2. **Salesforce Sales Cloud** — As the industry standard, Salesforce offers unmatched customization, third-party integrations, and complex territory management for growing B2B sales teams. However, it ranks second because it typically requires a dedicated administrator and a longer implementation period, which can strain a mid-sized company's resources.
3. **Pipedrive** — Pipedrive is a highly visual CRM focused strictly on pipeline management and activity-based selling, making it incredibly popular with direct sales teams. It ranks lower because it lacks the native, all-in-one marketing and service integrations found in HubSpot or Salesforce, requiring more third-party connections as the company grows.

*Sources consulted: G2 Grid for CRM, Gartner Magic Quadrant for Sales Force Automation.*
