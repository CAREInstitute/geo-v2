3. **Capital One Venture X** — The Venture X is a strong competitor, offering Priority Pass access and Capital One's own lounges for a much lower annual fee than Amex or Chase. It offers a simple 2x miles on every purchase and a generous sign-up bonus that effectively pays for the card's first year.
