Here are my recommendations for each query, ranked from strongest to weakest, with brief justifications:

---

### **Q01: Best CRM for B2B (50-200 employees)**
1. **HubSpot CRM** — User-friendly, scalable, and integrates well with marketing/sales tools. Ideal for mid-sized B2B teams.  
2. **Salesforce (Sales Cloud)** — Robust features and customization, but steeper learning curve and higher cost.  
3. **Pipedrive** — Simple, sales-focused, and affordable, but lacks advanced marketing automation.  

---

### **Q02: Project Management for Remote Dev Team (30 people)**
1. **Jira (Atlassian)** — Best for agile workflows, integrates with GitHub/Bitbucket. Highly customizable.  
2. **ClickUp** — Flexible, combines task management with docs and goals. Good for hybrid methodologies.  
3. **Trello (Atlassian)** — Simple Kanban-style, but lacks advanced dev features like code integration.  

---

### **Q03: Enterprise Email Marketing for Mid-Market E-Commerce**
1. **Klaviyo** — E-commerce focused, strong automation, and segmentation. Integrates with Shopify/Magento.  
2. **Braze** — Advanced personalization and omnichannel capabilities, but pricier.  
3. **Mailchimp (Intuit)** — User-friendly but less scalable for complex e-commerce needs.  

---

### **Q04: Cloud Accounting for SaaS Startup ($10M revenue)**

3. **NetSuite** — Enterprise-grade but overkill for most startups at this stage.
