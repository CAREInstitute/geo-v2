For a mid-market enterprise of 2,000 employees, an Identity and Access Management (IAM) solution must offer robust Single Sign-On (SSO), seamless lifecycle management, and reliable multi-factor authentication (MFA). It is incredibly reliable, user-friendly for employees, and scales perfectly for a 2,000-person company looking for a vendor-neutral identity provider.

Choosing the right platform largely comes down to whether you want a standalone identity provider or one tied to your existing device management or email ecosystem.
