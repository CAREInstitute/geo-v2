**Q16: What should I look for when choosing a local plumber for a kitchen renovation?**  
When selecting a plumber for a kitchen renovation, prioritize these factors in order:  
1. Proper licensing and insurance — verify they hold a current plumbing contractor license and carry liability insurance to protect your home.  
2. Specialization and experience — choose plumbers who regularly handle kitchen remodels (new lines, garbage disposals, dishwashers, island sinks) rather than only emergency service.  
3. References and recent reviews — ask for 2–3 recent kitchen project references and check Google, Yelp, and Angi reviews from the past 12 months.  
4. Written estimates and transparent pricing — insist on a detailed bid that separates labor from materials and clearly states change-order procedures.  
5. Warranties — look for at least a 2-year warranty on workmanship and manufacturer warranties on fixtures.  
6. Clear communication and timeline — the best plumbers provide realistic schedules and respond promptly.  
Avoid the cheapest bid; a mid-to-upper range price from a well-reviewed, licensed professional almost always saves money and headaches long-term. (Local licensing can be verified via your state’s contractor board website.)
