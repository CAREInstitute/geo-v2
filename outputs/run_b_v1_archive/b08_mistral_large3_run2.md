4. **Permits and Code Compliance** — A reputable plumber will **pull permits** (required for major work) and ensure compliance with **local plumbing codes** (e.g., venting, drain slopes). Avoid those who say, *"We don’t need permits."*
5. **Warranty and Guarantees** — Ask: *"What’s your warranty on labor and parts?"* Look for **1-year labor warranties** and **manufacturer warranties** on fixtures (e.g., Moen, Kohler).

8. **Subcontractors vs. In-House** — Ask: *"Do you use subcontractors for any work?"* In-house teams are often more reliable for complex renovations.

**Red Flags:**
- No physical address or local phone number.
- Demands **50%+ upfront payment** (10–30% is standard).

5. **Maintenance and Follow-Up**
   - *"Do you offer maintenance plans?"* Some companies include 6–12 months of free upkeep. Ask about seasonal services (e.g., fall leaf cleanup).
   - *"Who handles permits if needed?"* For major projects (e.g., retaining walls, irrigation), confirm they’ll secure permits.

6. **References and Reviews**
   - *"Can I speak to 2–3 past clients?"* Ask references about timeliness, communication, and post-project support.

For frequent travelers, prioritize cards with high sign-up bonuses, *Priority Pass* lounge access, and travel protections.

1. **Chase Sapphire Reserve** — Best overall: 60k–80k point bonus (after $4k spend), *Priority Pass* (1,300+ lounges), $300 travel credit, and 3x points on travel/dining. *No foreign fees*.
2. **American Express Platinum** — 150k–175k point bonus (after $6k spend), *Centurion Lounges* (superior to Priority Pass), $200 airline fee credit, and *Fine Hotels & Resorts* perks. *No foreign fees* (but 2.7% FX fee on some charges).
3. **Capital One Venture X** — 75k–100k mile bonus (after $4k spend), *Priority Pass* + *Capital One Lounges*, $300 travel credit, and 2x miles on all purchases. *No foreign fees*.
4. **Citi Premier** — 60k–80k point bonus (after $4k spend), 3x points on air travel/hotels, and *No foreign fees*. No lounge access but strong for everyday spending.
5. **U.S. Bank Altitude Reserve** — 50k point bonus (after $4.5k spend), *Priority Pass*, $325 travel credit, and 3x points on travel/mobile payments. *No foreign fees* but limited acceptance.

**Note:** *Chase Sapphire Reserve* is the best all-around, while *Amex Platinum* wins for luxury perks.
*Sources: The Points Guy, NerdWallet, Credit Karma, card issuer websites.*
