**Q01: What is the best CRM software for a B2B company with 50 to 200 employees?**
When selecting a CRM for a mid-market B2B company, the primary considerations are scalability, pipeline visibility, integration capabilities, and ease of adoption for growing sales and marketing teams. The tools must support complex, multi-stakeholder sales cycles without overwhelming the users.

2. **Salesforce (Sales Cloud)** — Salesforce offers unmatched customization and an extensive ecosystem of third-party integrations, making it ideal for complex B2B sales processes. However, it requires significantly more time, budget, and specialized staff to configure and maintain at this size.

For a B2B company in the 50 to 200 employee range, HubSpot generally offers the best balance of power and usability, while Salesforce remains the go-to for highly complex enterprise requirements.
